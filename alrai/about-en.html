Who are we?

AlRai Food Industries

AlRai Food Industries Company is one of the leading companies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, established in 1968 in the village of Al-Shaharin Al-Ahsa in the Eastern Province under the management of Mohammed Musa Bukhamsin. The company has rapidly developed to become one of the most prominent producers and exporters of juices and dairy products in the Kingdom.

The factory has received an "A" rating from the Saudi Food and Drug Authority, reflecting its commitment to quality and food safety standards.

The company's products include a variety of juices, dairy products, ice cream, and ice cream cups and cones.
