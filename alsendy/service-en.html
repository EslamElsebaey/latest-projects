Zakat and tax advice

  1. home
  2. Zakat and tax advice

The most important professional services in this section are as follows:

Customer Registration

Register customers with the General Authority of Zakat and Income

Customer Registration

Preparing zakat returns, income tax, withholding tax, and value-added tax for our clients in accordance with the regulations, and submitting them in lists program

Respond to inquiries

Assisting our customers in responding to the inquiries of the General Authority for Zakat and Income

Customer Representation

Representing clients in the procedures of objection and appeal against zakat and tax assessments submitted by the General Authority for Zakat and Income

Financial Systems Configuration

Preparing the financial systems for clients and their approval of the zakat and tax systems issued by the General Authority for Zakat and Income

Zakat and tax certificates

Assisting our customers with the issuance of zakat and tax certificates

Reviewing the zakat and tax assessments

Reviewing the zakat and tax assessments for customers issued by the General Authority for Zakat and Income and preparing objections to them

Training and Development

Training and developing awareness and knowledge in the field of zakat and tax systems for our clients and providing them with new systems related to zakat and tax systems and studying their impact on them
