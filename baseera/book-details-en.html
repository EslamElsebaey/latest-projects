thinking movements

Thinking movements provide a practical approach to integrating metacognition into daily education and making it an integral part of its contents. Using simple, everyday language to apply the 26 key thinking movements from Forward Thinking to Focusing and Clearing, Thinking Moves Easy is accessible to students and provides approaches and tools that educators can incorporate into their curriculum.

Authorization Roger Sutcliffe, Tom Bilgestone, Jason Buckley
Translation Abeer Hammad
