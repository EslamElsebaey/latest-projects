Who are we?

AlRai Food Industries

AlRai Food Industries was established in 1968 in the city of Al-Ahsa in the Eastern Province. It quickly developed from a small dairy factory to become a major Saudi company producing a wide range of food products. The factory is now one of the most important producers and suppliers of dairy and juices in the Kingdom.

To become leaders in the food industry
