Russia... launching a military satellite into orbit

The Russian Ministry of Defense revealed the successful launch of a military satellite from the Plesetsk space base into orbit. The ministry said in a statement yesterday, Monday: “We launched the satellite to its position in orbit with the Russian Fregat accelerator unit at the appropriate time, and it was accepted into the control department of the ground means of the Russian Aerospace Forces,” according to the Russian Sputnik news agency.
