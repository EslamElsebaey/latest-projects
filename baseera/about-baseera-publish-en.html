Our Vision

To be the first reference in the Arab world to provide professional content in the field of philosophical thinking P4C in Arabic

Our Message

We seek to author, translate and publish professional content in the field of P4C philosophical thinking for all age groups in Arabic

Our Values

  • • Quality
  • • Specialization
  • • Professionalism

Our goals

  • Providing content that serves the objectives of teaching P4C philosophical thinking to Arabic speakers.

  • Providing content that contributes to the development of P4C philosophical thinking education programs in Arabic.

  • Publishing and translating foundational books and the latest references and publications specialized in the field of philosophical thinking P4C.

  • Spreading the culture of philosophical thinking P4C in the Arab world.

  • Provide content to develop the skills of facilitators in Arabic.

  • Encouraging innovative Arab production of P4C philosophical thinking content.
